Isle of Dogs(2018)

dir. Wes Anderson


In the future, an outbreak of canine flu leads the mayot of a Japanese city to banish all dogs to an island that's a garbage dump. The outcasts must soon embark on an epic journey when a 12-year-old boy arrives on the island to find his beloved pet.
